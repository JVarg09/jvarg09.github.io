Lore of Pogo Treaty

UPRISE OF COUNTRIES (2080)

After many years of countries not creating empires or uprises a few countries have uprised with their strong army invading everything which would be called: Pompano, Empire Of Eg2, Tucana, Magpiee, Kingdom Of Spiders, Polixia, and Alphaur

CREATION OF BUBU ALICE (2082)

The biggest countries decided to create an alliance known as "Bubu Alice" named after a water bottle and badly-spelled alliance. The alliance wont stay for long until conflicts broke out.

GREAT POMPANO WAR (2085)

Polixia wished to invade Pompano as it got "too strong" which he decided to team up with Empire Of Eg and Alphaur sending combined troops taking 2/3 of Pompano's army. Then the leader of Pompano decided to take out all of his reserves 110 million vs 300 million. At the end Luca won the war and no country was taken land from except Magpiee. (Magpiee was involved in it with Luca's side but kind of did nothing with his army)

NUCLEAR OR COLD WAR (2089)

The leader of Polixia went insane with more ideas to conquer the whole world so he decided to try and attack Empire Of Eg2 (his past ally and the one who helped him) for no reason. No one wanted nuclear bombs as they have signed a treaty against it. The leader of Polixia did not care went insane, became a furry emo, and sent all his nukes to the world except South America and some continents causing a major nuclear winter.

HUMANITY IS ALIVE (2112)

After many people were hiding underground in Europe and some of the continents that were struck with nukes the radiation eventually came off and people started to rebuild humanity once again.

UPRISE OF POGO TREATY COUNTRIES

New countries appeared in the world with land that was once owned by fallen empires in 2080. Patagoniad which is one of them their land wasnt really struck with nukes. Bambronini located in Europe. Valcora in Russia. Prusawia located in East Asia. Estotu in the Mediterranean. Lastly Ekolia in the United States.

CREATION OF POGO TREATY

The new countries decided to make an alliance to grow together and have some sort of protection. Every country mentioned in Uprise Of Pogo Treaty Countries was in this alliance. Some other small countries like Fish1 or Pompanos were also in it but are pretty small.
